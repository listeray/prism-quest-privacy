This data may be used for advertising, ad measurement, analytics, fraud prevention, security, and compliance purposes.

https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

https://admob.google.com/

We use purchase information only to provide app functionality, such as unlocking purchased content or removing ads. We do not sell purchase information.

https://policies.google.com/privacy

We do not sell your personal information.
